✦ Company History

Our Journey

From a tiny bedroom studio to a beloved custom-merch brand — here's how it all happened.

2032
🏠

The Bedroom

DollDaily started as a passion project — one sewing machine, a pile of felt, and a dream to create dolls that actually look like my original characters.

2038
🌐

Going Online

We opened our first commisions for booking and were completley booked out on custom dolls orders in under 48 hours. The community response was overwhelming.

2045
🏭

First Studio Space

With a growing team of five artists, we moved into our first dedicated studio — complete with a fabric library, printing press, and way too much glitter.

2053
🌏

Going Global

International shipping launched. DollDaily creations began reaching customers across 30+ countries, from Tokyo to Toronto to Nairobi.

2061
🤝

Brand Collaborations

Our first official brand collab: a limited-edition series with an indie game studio. Every piece sold out within minutes of launch.

2070
♻️

Sustainability Pledge

We committed to 100% recycled and organic materials across all product lines, becoming one of the first fully sustainable custom-merch brands.

2080
🎓

The DollDaily Acedemy

We launched craft workshops and online tutorials, teaching thousands of aspiring makers the art of custom doll and merch creation.

2099
✨

A New Chapter

As we close one century and open another, DollDaily continues to grow — still handmade, still personal, and still made with every bit of love we started with.

✦ Our Vision

A world where every person has a piece of art made just for them.

We believe custom creations shouldn't be a luxury — they should be accessible, joyful, and personal. Our vision is to be the go-to destination for anyone who wants to turn their imagination into something tangible and beautiful, whether it's a miniature figure of their persona or a sticker of their favourite OC.

We dream of a future where every gift tells a story, and every product carries the soul of the person who imagined it.

✦ Our Mission

To craft meaningful, custom-made products with care, skill, and heart.

Our mission is simple: take your ideas seriously and make them real. We combine the warmth of handcraft with the precision of professional production so that every doll, keychain, sticker, and print we create is something you'll treasure.

We're committed to fast turnarounds, transparent pricing, and genuine human connection — because behind every order is a person who trusted us with something special.
